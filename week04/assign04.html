This website is inspired by the "Incerto" book series by Nassim Nicholas Taleb. All the books of this series have to do with the unknowablility of the future. One of the books, "Antifragile," is named after a term coined by Taleb to describe something that not only is not affected by chaos, it is made better.

The purpose of this website is to discuss how to put Taleb's philosophy into practice, since his book is very high level. I have tried to be comprehensive with respect to the aspects of life that antifragility should be applied to, but this is a work in progress.

Use the left navigation to change to the various pages that have more information about becoming more antifragile. For now, most links are to books I am studying on the subject, but the self-sufficiency link will take you to some of my thoughts.
